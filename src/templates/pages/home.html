Getting Started

To get started, click the Login / Register (1) button at the top of the page.

  • Existing user? Enter your details to log in.
  • New here? Click "Sign Up" (2) and complete the form.

Additional Tutorials 

My Tickets

The "My Tickets" page provides access to most of Conductor's core functionality. From this page, you can:

  • Create new tickets (1)
  • View and manage existing tickets (2)
  • Mark tickets as resolved (3)
  • Delete tickets (4)
Additionally, you can click on a ticket's header to view more details and comments (5). From this view, you can also edit the ticket's information. Next to each section is a blue collapse arrow that can be used to hide the section as needed. Note, certain operations such as deleting a ticket or marking a ticket as resolved cannot be reversed, as such, you will be prompted with a confirmation dialog before these actions are completed.

If you are an administrator, this page will also include a "Pending Review" section that displays tickets awaiting review. Administrators have full control over all tickets, allowing them to perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, and Delete) operations on any ticket, regardless of the author.
