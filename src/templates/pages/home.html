Additional Tutorials 

My Tickets

The My Tickets page offers access to most of Conductor's core features. From this page, you can:

  • Create new tickets (1)
  • View and manage existing tickets (2)
  • Mark tickets as resolved (3)
  • Delete tickets (4)
  • Review Tickets (administrators only)

Each section has a blue collapse arrow (/) that allows you to hide or show the section as needed. Additionally, clicking a ticket's header opens up a detailed view, where you can view comments and additional information. From this detailed view, you can also edit the details of a ticket.

Note: Certain actions, such as deleting a ticket or marking it as resolved, cannot be undone. You will be prompted with a confirmation dialog before these actions are completed. Once a ticket is resolved, it can no longer be edited
